About DroneScope Media

We create aerial stories that help businesses, properties, and events stand out.

DroneScope Media is a South African drone production team focused on professional aerial photography, video, and inspection services. We combine safe flying, strong visuals, and fast delivery for every project.

Our work is built around clarity: clear communication, clear visuals, and clear results for each client.

Our story

Founded by experienced drone pilots and creative storytellers, DroneScope Media started with a simple idea: use aerial media to show places and moments from a fresh perspective. Today we serve clients across real estate, events, commercial, and inspection industries.

What we believe

  • Safety and compliance are the foundation of every shoot.
  • Great visuals should be delivered quickly and clearly.
  • Every flight should solve a real business need.

Mission and Vision

Mission

To deliver high-quality aerial media that supports sales, marketing, and technical review—with clear communication, reliable operations, and standout visuals.

Vision

To be the trusted drone partner for South African brands and property professionals who need compelling aerial content and dependable service.

Our Journey

  • 2020: The Beginning

    DroneScope Media was founded with a passion for aerial storytelling and a commitment to safe, professional drone operations.

  • 2022: First Major Projects

    We completed our first real estate shoots and event coverages, building a reputation for quality and reliability.

  • 2024: Expansion

    Added inspection services and expanded our team to meet growing demand for aerial media across South Africa.

  • 2026: Today

    Continuing to innovate with new techniques and technologies, serving clients in real estate, events, and commercial sectors.
